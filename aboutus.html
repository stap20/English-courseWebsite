Frequently Asked Questions

What is Notcourse ?

Notcourses helps you to earn money from your students and teachers under your ESL company.

Where do I teach ?

Teachers can teach from anywhere in the world with a computer and Internet access.

What is the difference between Notcourses and any other ESL companies ?

  • Notcourses helps you to build your own ESL company and earn from your students and the teachers under your ESL company.
  • Earning 50% from each student you teach and 10% from each teacher under your own built ESL company.
  • Being a part of a unique teaching institution.
  • Career development and building your own business without paying marketing, quality assurance, quality control cost and any other managerial cost.
  • Competitive rates of pay.
  • Excellent working conditions where you can work from any place and any time.
  • Long term and rolling contracts.
  • The teachers is to be promoted to build their own ESL company after teaching 700 students and earning 10% from each teacher under your own ESL company if they exceeded 90% feedback from their students as an aggregated feedback.
  • Notcourses has the largest number of online language-learning Students on the Internet, as we earn 50% of each student we have the ability to expand and enlarge our platform so We’ve built tools to give you exposure to this community and immediately match you up with new students.

Teachers Duties

  • Preparing classroom activities that match the type and model of the curriculum.
  • Teaching classes of adult learners using Notcourses curriculum, Notcourses Matrix and a Notcourses live video chat.
  • Attending 10 days unpaid training before boarding.
  • Monitoring students performance, attendance, and activities.
  • Monitoring daily online attendance for students.
  • Investigating and understanding causes for student absences.
  • Developing the language aptitude for the students as they navigate through the various levels.
  • Handling complaints, settling disputes and resolving grievances and conflicts or otherwise negotiating with others.
  • The teachers will have to demonstrate the required charisma and technical ability to fulfill the short and long term goals and objectives of Notcourses company.
  • You will be responsible for the academic development of small groups of adult learners in an online class setting.
  • You should use Notcourses teaching methodology, Notcourses curriculum and Notcourses online Matrix.
